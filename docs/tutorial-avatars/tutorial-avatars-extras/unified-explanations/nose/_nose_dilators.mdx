### Nose Dilation

***

> The general term that refers to the overall 
> dilation of the nose canal.

:::danger Still under construction!

This explanation is still being worked on. 
Information may change in the future.

:::

***

**Nose Dilation** is tied to 2 distinct shapes for each eye in 
**Unified Expressions**: 

* NoseDilation
* NoseConstrict

These shapes represent the *offset* from the 
default **Nose Dilation** on an avatar. Generally it is a good 
idea to have the avatar use the average nose diameter as the 
basis, and use these the two shapes to modify the basis as such:
