### Cheek Puff

***

> An expression that represents how much 
> the cheeks are puffed out.

***

### Anatomy

This expression does not have any anatomical reference to a 
facial muscle, and is simply an action created by the context of 
the face containing a differential pressure in the inside of the mouth. 

### Description

Cheek Puff represents the mouth containing excess air, 
liquids, or other objects that accentuate the volume of the cheeks.
