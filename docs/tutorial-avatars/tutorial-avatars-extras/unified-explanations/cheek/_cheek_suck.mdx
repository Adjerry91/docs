### Cheek Suck

***

> An expression that represents how much 
> the cheeks are sucked into the mouth.

:::danger Still under construction!

This explanation is still being worked on. 
Information may change in the future.

:::

***

This expression does not have any anatomical reference to a 
facial muscle, and is simple an action created by the context of 
the face containing a differential pressure in the inside of the mouth. 
In this context, Cheek Suck represents the mouth containing a vacuum.
