### Mouth Raiser

***

> An expression that represents how much the 
> mouth is being lifted by the lower lip pushing upwards.

***

### Anatomy

**Mouth Raiser** is based on the 
[mentalis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mentalis)'s 
action of tucking the lip corners' downwards.

**Mouth Raiser** is part of 
the <ContextModal src={require('./_mouth_general.mdx').default}><b>Mouth</b></ContextModal> 
shape group and *conditionally* part of the **Sad** expressions sub-group.

### Description

Mouth Raiser an expression that causes the lip corners to be 
pulled directly downwards, curving the lips parabolically to 
look sad.

### Relationship to Mouth Frown

Mouth Raiser is complemented by 'Mouth Frown' 
by raising the sad expression, 
giving a more stress or dissapointed emotion.
