### Mouth Lower Down

***

> An expression that represents how much the 
> mouth is being lowered by the lower mouth muscles.

***

### Anatomy

Mouth Lower Down is based on the 
[depressor labii inferioris](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Depressor_labii_inferioris_muscle)'s 
action of lowering and opening the lips and mouth.

Mouth Lower Down is part of 
the <ContextModal src={require('./_mouth_general.mdx').default}><b>Mouth</b></ContextModal> 
shape group. 

### Description

Mouth Lower Down is an expression that represents how much the lower lips and mouth 
are being opened. 

### Relationship to Mouth Upper Up

Mouth Lower Down is complemented by 'Mouth Upper Up' 
by helping fully bare the teeth.
