### Mouth Upper Up

***

> An expression that represents how much the 
> mouth is being lifted by the upper mouth muscles.

***

### Anatomy

**Mouth Upper Up** is based directly on the 
[levator labii superioris](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Levator_labii_superioris)'s 
action of raising and opening the upper part of the lips and mouth.

**Mouth Upper Up** is part of 
the <ContextModal src={require('./_mouth_general.mdx').default}><b>Mouth</b></ContextModal> 
shape group. 

### Description

Mouth Upper Up is an expression that is solely 
responsible for raising the lips and upper mouth 
upwards.

### Relationship to Mouth Upper Deepen

Mouth Upper Up is complemented by 'Mouth Upper Deepen', 
which causes the upper lip to be pushed laterally into the cheeks.

### Relationship to Nose Sneer

Mouth Upper Up is complemented by 'Nose Sneer', 
which causes the upper lip to be pulled up even more extremely.
