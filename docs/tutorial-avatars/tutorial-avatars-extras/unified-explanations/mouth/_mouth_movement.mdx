### Mouth Movements

***

> The general term that refers to how the 
> mouth gets moved horizontally.

***

### Anatomy

All Mouth Movements are based on the 
[orbicularis oris orbital](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orbicularis_oris_muscle), 
[levator anguli oris](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Levator_anguli_oris), and 
[buccinator](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buccinator_muscle) 
muscle groupings complex action of moving the lips 
left and right

All Mouth Movements are part of 
the <ContextModal src={require('./_mouth_general.mdx').default}><b>Mouth</b></ContextModal> 
shape group. 

### Description

The mouth can be moved Left to Right, and is also split up
in an Upper and Lower denomination to give the Upper and Lower 
mouth parts the ability to move horizontally independently from 
each other.

The following shapes represents these *Mouth Movements*:

* Mouth Upper Right
* Mouth Upper Left
* Mouth Lower Right
* Mouth Lower Left
