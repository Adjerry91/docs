### Mouth Corner Puller

***

> An expression that represents how much the 
> lip corner gets pulled into the cheek.

***

### Anatomy

**Mouth Corner Puller** is based on the 
[zygomaticus major](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zygomaticus_major_muscle)

**Mouth Corner Puller** is part of 
the <ContextModal src={require('./_mouth_general.mdx').default}><b>Mouth</b></ContextModal> 
shape group and part of the **Smile** expressions sub-group.

### Description

Mouth Corner Puller is the primary expression associated with 
smiling. The corners of the lip are pulled mostly laterally and the 
lips themselves are lifted somewhat vertically.

### Relationship to Mouth Corner Slant

Mouth Corner Puller is complemented by 'Mouth Corner Slant'. 
'Mouth Corner Slant' causes the corners of the lips to raise slightly, 
giving the mouth a slightly more parabolic shape.

### Relationship to Mouth Dimple

Mouth Corner Puller is complemented by 'Mouth Dimple'. 
'Mouth Dimple' causes the corners of the lips to get pushed back 
into the mouth and tightens the corners of the lip slightly 
giving the smile a slightly more cheeky and more cheery expression.

### Relationship to Mouth Upper Deepen

Mouth Corner Puller is complemented by 'Mouth Upper Deepen', 
which causes the upper lip to be pushed laterally into the 
cheeks whilst grinning.
