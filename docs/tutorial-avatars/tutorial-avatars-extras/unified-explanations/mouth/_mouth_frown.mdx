### Mouth Frown

***

> An expression that represents how much the 
> mouth frowns.

***

### Anatomy

**Mouth Frown** is based on the 
[depressor anguli oris](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Depressor_anguli_oris_muscle)'s 
action of tucking the lip corners' downwards.

**Mouth Frown** is part of 
the <ContextModal src={require('./_mouth_general.mdx').default}><b>Mouth</b></ContextModal> 
shape group and part of the **Sad** expressions sub-group.

### Description

Mouth Frown an expression that causes the lip corners to be 
pulled directly downwards, curving the lips parabolically to 
look sad.

### Relationship to Mouth Stretch

Mouth Frown is complemented by 'Mouth Stretch'. 
'Mouth Stretch' complements by making Mouth Frown 
give a more weary or sad expression.

### Relationship to Mouth Raiser

Mouth Frown is complemented by 'Mouth Raiser'. 
'Mouth Raiser' complements by raising the sad expression, 
giving a more stress or dissapointed emotion.
