### Mouth Upper Deepen

***

> An expression that represents how much the 
> upper lip and mouth pulls into the cheek.

***

### Anatomy

Mouth Upper Deepen is directly based on the 
[zygomaticus minor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zygomaticus_minor_muscle) 
muscle's action of pulling the upper part of the mouth laterally and slightly horizontally.

**Mouth Upper Deepen** is part of 
the <ContextModal src={require('./_mouth_general.mdx').default}><b>Mouth</b></ContextModal> 
shape group, and *conditionally* part of the Smile group.

### Description

Mouth Upper Deepen is an expression that pulls the 
upper lip laterally and slightly vertically into the cheek.

This shape is a *modifier* shape to 'Mouth Upper Up', and relies 
on the context of 'Mouth Upper Up'

### Relationship to Mouth Upper Up

Mouth Upper Deepen is a direct complement to 'Mouth Upper Up' 
by modifying the shape of 'Mouth Upper Up' by creating a more 
distressed expression.

### Relationship to Mouth Corner Puller

Mouth Upper Deepen is a direct complement to 'Mouth Corner Puller' 
by modifying the shape of 'Mouth Corner Puller' by helping create a more 
expressive smile.
